Informed Citizen

Laws preserve our general protection and secure our freedom as individuals from violence by others, organizations, and the government itself. Living in a democratic society all citizens should be aware of how they are impacted by these important laws.

Informed Citizen presents accessible and filtered content on current laws going on in your state. Make sure to fill in your state and possible interests.

Instructions: Make sure to select a state. It is optional to select topics. Hold down the Ctrl (windows) / Command (Mac) button to select multiple options.
